Sanghum Film Collective is an obsession with all things filmi— exploring mainstream, independent, and experimental films from the Indian subcontinent and its diasporas.

We are Rupali Morzaria, Sajdeep Soomal, and Aaditya Aggarwal, currently based in Tkaronto (Toronto) and Tiohti:áke (Montréal).
